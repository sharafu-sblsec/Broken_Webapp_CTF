The Tale of Kandanpoocha in Jail

Once upon a tail-flicking afternoon, in the Kingdom of Meowvalon, there lived a legendary feline banker named Kandanpoocha. Known across alleyways and attic vaults for founding the majestic poochaBANK, Kandanpoocha handled everything from tuna bonds to sardine securities. But with great fur came great suspicion.

One moonlit evening, as the Whisker Moon rose high, royal guards from the Feline Financial Authority came storming into poochaBANK. Kandanpoocha was accused of the greatest crime in catconomics history — unauthorized yarn laundering and overfeeding the royal kittens with interest-heavy fish treats.

With a puffed tail and proud whiskers, Kandanpoocha meowed, "I did it all for the cats!" But alas, he was sentenced to 7 lives in the Catnip Correctional Castle, a grim place where scratching posts were dull and fish bowls were strictly ornamental.

Yet, behind those metal grates and sleepy-eyed guards, Kandanpoocha began secretly teaching financial wisdom to his fellow feline inmates. He established the first underground litter-ledger, taught tabby-tax evasion awareness, and even ran a secret sardine exchange between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM (with proper grooming breaks in between).

Rumor has it that every full moon, you can still hear his soft meows echoing through the stone halls — "Mewwww meww mewww... diversify your tuna investments..."

So the next time you transfer some coins or request a fish loan, remember the sacrifice of Kandanpoocha — the cat who clawed against injustice and got purrsecuted for caring too much.
